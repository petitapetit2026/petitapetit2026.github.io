Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Spoiler: We don't sell your data.

📋 What We Collect

  • Location data (only during run tracking)
  • Running data (distance, time, pace, route)
  • Personal info (name, birthday, gender — only if you choose to provide)
  • Saved quotes (your favorite motivational quotes)
  • Purchase history (to restore your purchases)
  • Usage analytics (running time slots, feature usage)
  • Device region (for localized holiday greetings)

🚫 What We Don't Do

  • We don't sell your data. Ever.
  • We don't use your data for advertising.
  • We don't track you when you're not using the app.
  • We don't reveal your personal identity to third parties.

☁️ Your Data Storage

  • Running records sync to the cloud so you don't lose them when switching devices.
  • Saved quotes and purchase records also sync to the cloud.
  • Analytics data is processed to improve product experience.
  • You can delete your account and all data anytime in your profile.

📊 Analytics Usage

We collect anonymous usage data to:

  • Understand running habits of our users
  • Improve app features and experience
  • Fix issues and optimize performance

This data is not used to identify you personally.

🔗 Third-Party Services

We use the following services:

  • Cloud services (authentication, data storage, analytics)
  • Google Sign-In (optional login method)
  • Apple Sign-In (optional login method)

These services have their own privacy policies.

🔔 Notifications

With your permission, we send local notifications for:

  • Birthday greetings (if you set your birthday)
  • Holiday running reminders

You can disable notifications anytime in Settings.

🎯 Live Activity

During runs, we display your progress on Dynamic Island and Lock Screen. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted.
